Our Services

Complete dental care, under one roof.

From routine check-ups to advanced implants and smile makeovers — preventive, cosmetic and surgical dentistry delivered with warmth and the latest technology.

All Dental Services

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements that restore full bite strength and confidence — a lifelong solution for missing teeth.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless, often single-sitting root canals using rotary endodontics — saving your natural tooth and ending the ache.

Veneers & Crowns

Transform your smile with custom porcelain veneers and durable, lifelike ceramic crowns that look completely natural.

Teeth Cleaning & Scaling

Professional ultrasonic scaling and polishing to remove plaque and tartar — keeping gums healthy and breath fresh.

Teeth Whitening

Safe, professional whitening that lifts years of stains in a single visit — for a brighter, more confident smile.

Braces & Invisalign

Straighten crooked or crowded teeth with metal, ceramic or clear aligners — a healthier bite and a better smile.

Dentures & Bridges

Comfortable, well-fitted dentures and fixed bridges to replace missing teeth and restore natural chewing and speech.

Fillings & Sealants

Tooth-coloured composite fillings and protective sealants that treat cavities and guard against future decay.

Tooth Extractions

Gentle, safe removal of decayed, damaged or wisdom teeth — with careful aftercare advice for a smooth recovery.

Gum Disease Treatment

Periodontal care to treat bleeding, swollen gums and prevent tooth loss — restoring the foundation of your smile.

Paediatric Dentistry

Gentle, kid-friendly care that builds positive dental habits from the very first visit — for healthy young smiles.

Oral & Minor Surgery

Wisdom-tooth removal and minor oral surgical procedures performed safely under strict sterilisation protocols.

Mouth & Night Guards

Custom-fitted guards to protect teeth from grinding (bruxism) and during sports — comfortable and durable.

24×7 Emergency Care

Toothache, broken tooth or dental trauma? Call us — we keep same-day slots open and we'll get you in fast.

FAQs

Questions, answered

Everything patients usually ask before their first visit.

Are your dental treatments painless?

Yes. We use modern local anaesthesia and rotary instruments so that procedures like root canals, fillings and extractions are comfortable and virtually painless.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

Both work. Booking ahead by phone, WhatsApp or our online form keeps your wait short, but walk-ins are welcome — and we always keep same-day slots open for dental emergencies.

How much does treatment cost?

It depends on the treatment and your specific case. We always provide a clear written estimate before starting any work, with no hidden charges. Call +91 80776 45073 for a quote.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely. Our paediatric dentistry is gentle and kid-friendly, helping children build positive dental habits from their very first visit.

What are your clinic timings?

We're open Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM and 4:30 PM – 8:00 PM. The clinic is closed on Sundays.
